ReelMeal

Save recipes from your favorite food videos

Features

Import from Anywhere

Save recipes from TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and websites. AI automatically extracts ingredients and instructions from video captions.

Organize Your Recipes

Create custom cookbooks to organize your collection. Mark favorites for quick access and filter by cuisine, difficulty, or cook time.

Smart Shopping Lists

Auto-generate shopping lists from recipes. Consolidate ingredients, adjust quantities, and check off items as you shop.

Cook with Confidence

Step-by-step instructions, ingredient scaling for any serving size, and progress tracking while you cook.

How It Works

1. Share a Video

Find a recipe video on TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube. Tap Share and select ReelMeal.

2. AI Does the Rest

Our AI extracts the recipe details, ingredients, and cooking instructions automatically.

3. Cook & Enjoy

Follow the recipe, create shopping lists, and save to your cookbooks for later.

4. Share Cookbooks

Create and share cookbooks with friends and family. Sync across all your devices with iCloud.
