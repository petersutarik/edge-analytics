Privacy Policy

ClipCook

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Edge Analytics ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ClipCook mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple, we collect:

  • Your Apple ID identifier (for account authentication)
  • Your name (if you choose to share it)
  • Your email address (if you choose to share it)

This information is used solely to identify your account and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

User-Created Content

We store the content you create within the app, including:

  • Recipes you save (titles, ingredients, instructions, notes)
  • Cookbooks you create
  • Shopping lists
  • Your preferences and settings

Photos

When you use the camera or photo library to import a recipe, the selected photo is sent to our servers for AI-powered recipe extraction. Photos are processed temporarily and are not stored permanently on our servers.

Temporary Processing Data

When you import a recipe from a video platform, we temporarily process:

  • The URL you share with the app
  • Video metadata (title, description, captions)
  • Audio transcriptions for recipe extraction

This data is processed to extract recipe information and is not stored permanently on our servers.

AI Chat Messages

When you use the Sous Chef feature, your messages are sent to AI services for processing. Chat history is stored locally on your device and is not retained on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide and maintain the core features of ClipCook
  • Recipe Extraction: To automatically extract recipe details from videos and photos using AI
  • AI Assistant: To provide recipe suggestions and cooking guidance via the Sous Chef feature
  • Sync: To synchronize your data across your devices via iCloud
  • Subscription Management: To manage your subscription status

Third-Party Services

ClipCook uses the following third-party services:

Google Gemini AI

Used for recipe extraction, audio transcription, and AI-powered cooking assistance. Video metadata, transcriptions, photos, and chat messages are sent to Google's servers for processing. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Apify

Used to retrieve video content from supported platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) when you import a recipe via URL. The URL you provide is sent to Apify's servers. See Apify's Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat

Used for subscription management. RevenueCat processes subscription-related data including purchase history. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple iCloud

Used for data synchronization across your devices. Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

Your recipe data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via Apple's iCloud service. All data sent to external services is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. You can delete your recipes, cookbooks, and shopping lists at any time within the app. If you delete the app, your local data will be removed from your device.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your recipe data
  • Opt out of optional data collection

Children's Privacy

ClipCook is rated for ages 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly and does not contain any inappropriate content.

Analytics and Tracking

ClipCook does not use analytics services, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies. We do not track your behavior for advertising purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
